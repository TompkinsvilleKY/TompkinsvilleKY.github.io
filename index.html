The domain registration for cityoftompkinsville.com had expired.

After seeing this domain posted across various sites (such as the BRADD city officials directory, the Tompkinsville Fire Department Yellowpages listing and the Facebook page for the Tompkinsville Sewer Plant) we decided to renew the domain and build a proper website for our town. We're a fantastic town with a poor web presence, but we're working to change this.

We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused you, and will ensure that future searches for information on our fair city will be met swiftly and accurately.
